Frequently Asked Questions

What is an SSL certificate?

An SSL certificate is a digital certificate that authenticates a website's identity and enables an encrypted connection.

How long does it take to issue a certificate?

DV certificates are issued instantly. OV certificates take 1-2 days, while EV certificates typically take 3-5 days for thorough verification.

Can I upgrade my certificate?

Yes, you can upgrade from DV to OV or EV certificates at any time. Contact our support team for assistance.
